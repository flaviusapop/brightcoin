What is BrightCOIN

We are a technology platform that enables entrepreneurs to create Utility or Security Token Offerings (ICO or STO). You can create a coin, setup your smart contract, and raise funds in a legally compliant manner. There are ZERO upfront fees & there’s no coding required.

BrightCOIN can create utility as well as asset backed and non asset backed security tokens. These tokens can represent equity, debt, revenue share, profit participation, utility and much more.

How it Works

Issue and distribute your tokens

With the simple click of a button you can distribute your tokens to contributors, advisors, founders, employees, etc. You can also list your tokens on our partner exchange and get liquidity immediately following your ICO.

Step 1

Create a token & setup your offering

Create your coin using our simple coin creating wizard. You can customize all elements of your coin including name, symbol and price. Next, you'll setup your token campaign; you decide the start/end dates, bonuses, rewards, caps and more. Our friendly editor explains all the steps involved in great detail.

Step 2

Launch your token offering

Once you setup your token offering we'll give you all the links required to launch. Links include signup and login pages for your contributors (with instructions on how to fund your coin). You simply hyperlink our urls to your landing page and you're ready to start collecting funds! All the details of the token offering are managed by our platform.

Step 3

Issue and distribute your tokens

With the simple click of a button you can distribute your tokens to contributors, advisors, founders, employees, etc. You can also list your tokens on our partner exchange and get liquidity immediately following your token offering.

Regulatory Compliance

The BrightCOIN platform adheres to all rules and regulations regarding Security and Utility tokens. In the U.S we create and distribute Security tokens in compliance with Reg D 506c, Reg S and Reg A+. This includes Accredited Investor verification. Outside of the U.S we can create and distribute both Utility and Security tokens. BrightCOIN is GDPR compliant.

Our “Programable securities” enable compliance not only during your token offering, but also during secondary exchange trading. You can set BrightCoin to adhere to all rules and regulations regarding securities and utility tokens.

Furthermore, Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti Money Laundering (AML) are included in our service. We comply with the strictest standards of money laundering laws including:

Sanctions, terror watch, PEP & adverse media list checks

KYC/AML Aware: Transfer tokens to investors that are KYC/AML verified

Photo ID and passport verification

Jurisdictionally Aware: Geofenced transfer rights ensure investors from blocked countries can’t own your token

Selfie & Video ID verification

Accreditation Aware: Enable or disable transfer of tokens to investors based on their accreditation status

Investor AWARE: Restrict participation and transfer of tokens based on number of investors
